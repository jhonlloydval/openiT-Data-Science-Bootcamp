09 — Usage Rules
Do's & Don'ts
Brand consistency rules for applying ToolHaive AI's visual identity across screens, slides, and print.
✓ DO
  • →Use the logo on dark, light, navy, or gradient backgrounds only
  • →Always pair the icon with the wordmark in headers
  • →Use Syne for all headings and display text
  • →Use DM Mono for all labels, tags, and code contexts
  • →Apply gradient left-to-right or top-left-to-bottom-right only
  • →Maintain minimum 32px clear space around the logo
  • →Use hexagonal motifs in backgrounds at low opacity (≤6%)
✗ DON'T
  • ×Never stretch or distort the hexagonal icon
  • ×Never use the gradient on busy photographic backgrounds
  • ×Never mix Syne with system fonts (Arial, Inter, Roboto)
  • ×Never rotate the gradient to diagonal 45° right
  • ×Never use colors outside the approved palette
  • ×Never place white text on the cream (#E5E5BE) background
  • ×Never add drop shadows or glow effects to the logo

# The Dragon Who Loved Flowers

In a mountain cave lived a dragon named Ember. Everyone in the kingdom feared him because he was enormous and breathed fire. But Ember had a secret: he did not want treasure. He loved flowers.

Every night, he flew down to the valley and gently warmed the frozen soil with his fire breath. Soon, rare blue roses bloomed where nothing had grown before.

One morning, a brave princess named Aria followed the trail of blue roses and found Ember planting seeds with his claws.

“You are not a monster,” she said.

Ember lowered his head. “I only wanted to make the valley beautiful.”

Princess Aria invited Ember to the kingdom, and the people slowly learned not to fear him. Ember became the royal gardener, using his warm breath to grow flowers through winter.

And from then on, whenever blue roses bloomed, the people remembered that even dragons can have gentle hearts.

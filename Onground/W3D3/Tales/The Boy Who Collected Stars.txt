# The Boy Who Collected Stars

In the year 3042, cities floated above the clouds and children traveled to school in tiny silver spacecraft. But a lonely boy named Eli spent his nights on the roof of his apartment, staring at the stars.

One evening, he noticed a small glowing object falling from the sky. It landed softly beside him — a broken star drone from deep space.

Eli repaired it using spare wires and old batteries. When the drone woke up, it projected a map of forgotten galaxies onto the night sky.

“You have restored me,” said the drone. “I can take you anywhere.”

Together, Eli and the little machine traveled across colorful planets, crystal moons, and oceans made of light. But after every journey, Eli always returned home before sunrise.

Years later, Eli became the greatest explorer in the galaxy. Yet he never forgot the tiny broken drone that taught him the universe was much bigger — and kinder — than he had imagined.

# The Moonlit Seed

Once upon a time, in a quiet village beside a silver lake, a little girl named Mina found a glowing seed under the moonlight. She planted it beside her cottage and watered it every night with kindness and songs.

By morning, the seed had grown into a tree with golden leaves. At the top of the tree was a tiny door. Mina climbed up and knocked gently.

A fairy opened the door and smiled. “You cared for something small without knowing what it would become. For that, you may make one wish.”

Mina thought carefully. She did not ask for jewels or a palace. Instead, she wished that no one in her village would ever go hungry.

From that day on, the golden tree grew fruit in every season. The villagers shared it with one another, and Mina learned that the best magic begins with a kind heart.
